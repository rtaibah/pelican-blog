5 Linux Commercials I Like

Published: Tue 05 August 2008
By rami_taibah

In blog.

  • Advocacy tags:

Since this is a *Linux advocacy* blog, it's only normal for me to share with you guys my favorite list of *Linux* commercials. I love these because of the deep messages and meanings behind each of them: ## 1-Float Like A Butterfly Muhammed Ali and *Linux* have many things in common, his quick fists and swift feet are akin to *Linux*'s flexibility and speed. Ali is the greatest boxer in history, and to me, *Linux* is the greatest OS! Indeed Ali claimed he is the greatest boxer, and he is probably right about *Linux* also. Watch the great legend promote openness: [youtube BesI6NEPWlM] ## 2-Novell Knows Spoofing I know I know, we all grew bored of these "I am a Mac I am a PC" commercials, but you gotta admit they are quite clever. Here are two from Novell [youtube rtp5gNhBZgo] [youtube 7eTguZ5OzJ4] ## 3-Then Linux Wins A strong message, and true in every sense. [youtube uBUgEx_91BU] ## 4-IBM: The New Allie [youtube KwEWxpOWOok] ## 5-The Boy Has Been Adopted [youtube RJ3eq_lKHFk]
