Photobucket Back Online in Saudi Arabia

Published: Sat 01 March 2008
By rami_taibah

In blog.

Last week I [reported][1] that Saudi Arabia has blocked [photobucket][2] for some peculiar reason. This censorship coincided with Pakistan's blocking of youtube. Anyhow I am happy to inform you that [photobucket][2] was unblocked today. Did my E-mail resonate with the authorities? I really don't know. I would like to think so! But in any case I am sure am not the only one escalated the issue. [1]: /blog/internet/photobucket-blocked-in-ksa/ [2]: http://www.photobucket.com
