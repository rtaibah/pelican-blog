"StumbleUpon Whooping Digg's Ass!"

Published: Sat 26 January 2008
By rami_taibah

In blog.

tags: StumbleUpon Digg Social-Media Problogger

Problogger is currently running a poll asking "What is your favorite social bookmarking site." I took a quick glance at the list and thought, yeah Digg is gonna win this. I clicked on Mixx and submitted my vote. But to my amazement I found that StumbleUpon is ahead of Digg by a whopping 150 votes! What's interesting is that Problogger's main audience are bloggers who probably use social bookmarking for promoting their blogs. So what makes them prefer StumbleUpon over Digg? I don't wanna get ahead of myself, but I have my thoughts on that and I will keep them for myself until Problogger's poll is finished. So keep in tune!!
